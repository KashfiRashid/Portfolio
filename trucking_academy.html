Addressing workforce retention through learning & community

the challenge.

Client believed they had a marketing problem.

"We have a loyal customer base, but it isn't growing due to our outdated online presence."

Our diagnosis: Through ethnographic research, we discovered the real issue was an industry-wide worker shortage. The trucking industry was losing the competition for Gen-Z talent.

research process.

10 real truck drivers interviewed.

Created 3 detailed personas (Fleet Manager, Solo Operator, Junior Driver). Mapped user journeys identifying key pain points. Hosted participatory workshop with 8 participants (0-20 years experience).

impact.

Addressing the root cause.

By conducting workshops with real truck drivers and iterating based on their feedback, we created a solution that addresses the diagnosed problem rather than surface symptoms.

Expected outcomes: Lower barriers to industry entry, accelerated skill development, sustainable community engagement, and direct employer-candidate connections.
