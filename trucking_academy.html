Trucking
Academy

Addressing workforce retention through learning & community

the challenge.

Client believed they had a marketing problem.

"We have a loyal customer base, but it isn't growing due to our outdated online presence."

Our diagnosis: Through ethnographic research, we discovered the real issue was an industry-wide worker shortage. The trucking industry was losing the competition for Gen-Z talent.

research process.

10 real truck drivers interviewed.

Created 3 detailed personas (Fleet Manager, Solo Operator, Junior Driver). Mapped user journeys identifying key pain points. Hosted participatory workshop with 8 participants (0-20 years experience).

key insights.

Pain points → Solutions.

01
Convoluted learning paths
Complex certification system creates confusion about skill progression
→
Linear progression system
Clear, color-coded courses with visual progress tracking
02
Dense, inaccessible resources
Overwhelming textbooks lacking practical context
→
Bite-sized content
Quick quizzes and instant feedback for busy professionals
03
High training costs
Uncertainty about career prospects creates barrier to entry
→
Community support
Connect with experienced drivers nationwide for guidance

the solution.

Comprehensive learning platform.

Trucking Academy - A mobile app that streamlines the path to a lucrative trucking career through guided learning, community mentorship, and direct employment connections.

📚
Guided Learning Path
Linear, color-coded courses with visual progress tracking

👥

Connect with experienced drivers nationwide for mentorship

user journey.

Ben's path to success.

Following Ben, a 28-year-old delivery driver seeking upward mobility, through his journey from frustration to achieving his Class 3 license.

design process.

From wireframes to high-fidelity.

Iterative design process based on user feedback and workshop insights. Focus on clarity, accessibility, and engagement.

final design.

Mobile app delivered.

Complete learning platform with onboarding, courses, practice quizzes, voice-matching for community calls, forums, and job board integration.

impact.

Addressing the root cause.

By conducting workshops with real truck drivers and iterating based on their feedback, we created a solution that addresses the diagnosed problem rather than surface symptoms.

Expected outcomes: Lower barriers to industry entry, accelerated skill development, sustainable community engagement, and direct employer-candidate connections.
